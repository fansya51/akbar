Choosing the right hiking boots depends on several factors: the type of terrain you'll be hiking on, the duration of your hikes, weather conditions, and your personal comfort preferences. For light day hikes on well-maintained trails, trail runners or light hiking shoes may be sufficient. For more challenging terrain or multi-day hikes with a heavy pack, mid to high-cut boots with good ankle support are recommended. Our product descriptions include detailed recommendations for each boot's intended use.

To maintain the waterproof properties of your gear, regular cleaning and proper care are essential. For waterproof jackets and pants, wash them according to the care instructions (typically gentle cycle with technical wash detergent). Avoid fabric softeners as they can damage the waterproof membrane. After washing, reactivate the DWR (Durable Water Repellent) coating by tumble drying on low heat or using a specialized reproofing spray. For waterproof footwear, clean mud and dirt after each use, and periodically treat with a waterproofing product designed for the specific material of your boots.

Backpack capacity recommendations based on trip duration:

  • Day hikes: 15-30 liters - Enough for water, snacks, rain gear, first aid, and a few personal items
  • 1-3 night trips: 30-50 liters - Room for sleeping bag, small tent, cooking equipment, clothes, and food
  • 3-5 night trips: 50-70 liters - Additional capacity for more food, clothes, and possibly more rugged gear
  • Extended trips (5+ nights): 70+ liters - Space for additional supplies, food, and potentially specialized equipment

Remember that your individual gear choices, season of travel, and personal packing style may affect the ideal capacity for your needs.

Warranty & Returns

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on unused items in original condition with all tags and packaging. To initiate a return, log into your account and select the order containing the item(s) you wish to return. Follow the prompts to generate a return shipping label. Return shipping is free for exchanges, and a flat fee of $7.95 for returns. Once we receive your return, please allow 5-7 business days for processing. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method.

To submit a warranty claim, please visit our Warranty Claim page and complete the form with your order information, product details, and a description of the defect or issue. Please include clear photos showing the problem. Our warranty team will review your claim within 3-5 business days and contact you with next steps. Most products carry a 1-year warranty against manufacturing defects. High-end equipment and select brands may offer extended warranties of up to 3 years or lifetime guarantees, as specified in the product description.

Yes, most sale items are eligible for returns within 30 days, but special markdown items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned unless defective. Clearance items are eligible for exchange or store credit only. Please check the product page for any specific return restrictions before purchasing. If you're uncertain about an item's return eligibility, please contact our customer service team for clarification before making your purchase.
